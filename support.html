    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate are the soil recommendations?
    Our soil recommendations are based on analysis of thousands of soil samples and agricultural research. For best results, we recommend conducting a soil test for precise nutrient levels.
    Can I use AgriSmart for organic farming?
    Absolutely! AgriSmart provides specific recommendations for organic farming practices, including natural pest control methods, organic fertilizers, and sustainable irrigation techniques.
    How often should I update my farm data?
    We recommend updating your farm data at least once per season or whenever you make significant changes to your crops or land. For real-time monitoring, connect your sensors for automatic updates.
    Does AgriSmart work in all climate conditions?
    Yes, AgriSmart adapts recommendations based on local climate data. Our system considers temperature, humidity, rainfall patterns, and seasonal variations to provide location-specific advice.
